About Us

Tudela is a charming municipality located in the beautiful Camotes Islands, Cebu. Known for its breathtaking natural wonders, Tudela is home to lush mountains, crystal-clear seas, and captivating caves like the famous Bukilat Cave in Barangay McArthur. Although it doesn’t have sandy beaches, Tudela offers scenic views of the sea, rolling hills, and a laid-back island vibe that’s perfect for those who want to unwind and connect with nature.

Our town takes pride in its rich history, vibrant culture, and warm, welcoming people. The iconic century-old acacia tree in the town center stands as a living witness to Tudela’s heritage and community spirit.

Tudela is also known for its agricultural produce and unique local delicacies. Visitors can enjoy exploring natural attractions, discovering hidden caves, experiencing traditional festivities, and learning about the stories that make Tudela special.

Whether you’re a traveler seeking adventure or a guest longing for peace and quiet, Tudela, Camotes welcomes you to explore, experience, and enjoy the best of island life.
