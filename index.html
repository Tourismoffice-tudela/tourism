About Tudela

Tudela is one of the four municipalities in the Camotes Islands, part of Cebu Province in the Philippines. Known for its natural attractions, friendly locals, and vibrant culture, Tudela offers a peaceful retreat for travelers who want to experience authentic island life.

From majestic caves like Bukilat Cave to serene coastal views, Tudela is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.
